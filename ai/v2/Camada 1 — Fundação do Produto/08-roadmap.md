# Specforge V2 Roadmap

## Status

Accepted

## Purpose

This document defines the long-term evolution of Specforge.

The roadmap exists to provide direction rather than deadlines.

Its purpose is to ensure that future development remains aligned with the Vision, Product DNA, Core Principles and Constraints of the project.

This roadmap is intentionally outcome-oriented rather than feature-oriented.

The goal is not to ship features.

The goal is to progressively improve the quality of knowledge preservation and AI context generation.

---

# Roadmap Philosophy

Most roadmaps focus on adding capabilities.

Specforge focuses on increasing understanding.

Every stage of the roadmap should improve one or more of the following:

- Knowledge Quality
- Context Quality
- Traceability
- AI Consistency
- Developer Confidence

Feature count is not a success metric.

Context quality is.

---

# V1 — Specification Generation

## Theme

From Idea to Specification.

## Goal

Provide developers with a structured way to transform ideas into implementation-ready specifications.

## Core Capabilities

### Specification Generation

Generate:

- Requirements
- Design Documents
- Architecture Documents
- Contracts
- Tasks

### Harness Generation

Generate AI guidance documents.

### Clarification

Ask questions to improve generated outputs.

## Outcome

Idea
↓
Specs
↓
AI
↓
Code

## Limitations

Knowledge preservation is still limited.

The system generates specifications but does not yet treat knowledge as a first-class asset.

---

# V2 — Knowledge First Architecture

## Theme

From Specifications to Knowledge.

## Goal

Transform knowledge into the primary asset of the system.

Specifications become derived artifacts.

## Core Capabilities

### Knowledge Layer

Introduce:

- Vision
- Product DNA
- Principles
- Constraints
- Assumptions
- Risks

### Decision Records

Store:

- Context
- Decisions
- Alternatives
- Rationale
- Tradeoffs

### Discoveries

Preserve product and engineering discoveries.

### Knowledge Lifecycle

Conversation
↓
Discovery
↓
Decision
↓
Knowledge
↓
Specification
↓
Harness

### Context Packages

Generate reusable AI-ready context.

### Knowledge Preservation Engine

Prevent loss of rationale and intent.

## Outcome

Developers move from Specification Driven to Knowledge Driven development.

---

# V3 — Knowledge Intelligence

## Theme

Understanding Relationships.

## Goal

Enable Specforge to understand connections between knowledge artifacts.

## Core Capabilities

### Knowledge Graph

Connect:

- Decisions
- Discoveries
- Constraints
- Requirements
- Architecture

### Relationship Mapping

Understand:

- dependencies
- influences
- conflicts
- implications

### Impact Analysis

Answer:

"What happens if this decision changes?"

### Contradiction Detection

Identify conflicts across project knowledge.

### Knowledge Health

Measure:

- completeness
- consistency
- coverage

## Outcome

Knowledge becomes navigable rather than merely stored.

---

# V4 — Context Intelligence

## Theme

Optimizing Context.

## Goal

Automatically generate higher quality context packages.

## Core Capabilities

### Context Scoring

Evaluate context quality.

### Context Compression

Reduce tokens without losing meaning.

### Context Optimization

Identify missing information.

### Context Recommendations

Suggest improvements.

### Knowledge Gap Detection

Identify missing knowledge.

## Outcome

Developers spend less time preparing context.

AI receives better context automatically.

---

# V5 — AI Development Operating System

## Theme

Knowledge as Infrastructure.

## Goal

Transform Specforge into a foundational layer for AI-assisted development.

## Core Capabilities

### Universal Context Layer

Provide standardized context for any AI provider.

### Multi-Agent Support

Coordinate multiple AI agents using shared knowledge.

### Provider Abstraction

Support evolving AI ecosystems.

### Project Intelligence

Continuously improve project understanding.

## Outcome

Specforge becomes the system responsible for preserving and distributing project knowledge across AI workflows.

# Explicit Non-Goals

The roadmap does not include:

- Project Management
- Sprint Planning
- Ticket Management
- Time Tracking
- AI Billing
- Proprietary Hosting
- Vendor Lock-In

---

# Roadmap Evaluation Framework

1. Does this improve knowledge preservation?
2. Does this improve context quality?
3. Does this improve traceability?
4. Does this improve AI consistency?
5. Does this respect Product DNA?
6. Does this respect Constraints?

---

# Long-Term Vision

The long-term goal of Specforge is not to become a better specification generator.

The long-term goal is to become the most effective system for transforming human knowledge into AI-ready context.

Specifications are one output of that process.

Knowledge remains the foundation.

---

# Final Statement

The future of software development will increasingly depend on AI.

The future of AI-assisted development will increasingly depend on context.

Specforge exists to ensure that context is treated as a first-class engineering asset.
