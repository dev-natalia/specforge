# Purpose

This document defines the Product Profile used by Progressive Specification.

Unlike Story and Feature, Product is not a new specification format.

Instead, Product Mode acts as an orchestration profile that activates the complete Specforge V2 architecture.

The purpose of this document is to define how Progressive Specification integrates with the existing Knowledge First ecosystem established in V2.

---

# Overview

Story and Feature introduce new workflows optimized for smaller scopes.

Product does not.

Product represents the full engineering process originally established by Specforge V2.

When Product scope is selected, the Progressive Specification Engine delegates execution to the complete V2 generation architecture.

---

# Core Principle

Story and Feature simplify.

Product preserves.

---

Product Mode should not reduce rigor.

Product Mode should not reduce governance.

Product Mode should not reduce knowledge discovery.

Its purpose is to preserve the complete engineering planning process required for complex systems.

# Product Philosophy

The Product Profile assumes that:

- Significant uncertainty exists.
- Discovery is required.
- Architectural decisions matter.
- Knowledge preservation matters.
- Long-term maintainability matters.

The objective is not rapid implementation.

The objective is sustainable engineering execution.

# Closing Statement

The Product Profile exists to ensure that Progressive Specification does not replace the original Specforge vision.

Instead, it extends it.

Story and Feature provide lightweight pathways for smaller initiatives.

Product preserves the full Knowledge First architecture for complex systems.

Together, these profiles allow Specforge to adapt its process to the scale of engineering work while maintaining a unified philosophy, architecture, and knowledge model.
